Chicken with peppers

Ingredients:

Chicken breasts (1-2lbs)
2 bell peppers, sliced in strips
4 small tomatoes, deseeded and chopped
4 cooked pieces of turkey bacon
1 small chopped onion
1 can of chicken broth
1 tbspn of sugar
1/4 cup of flour
Rice

Directions:

1. Cut chicken into strips and brown in skillet. (If you have a George Foreman, you can easily cook the chicken on there. It's an easy clean up as well.)
2. In a separate pan, sautee onion until slightly brown. Add bell peppers and cook 10 minutes on high being careful to stir.
3. Add tomatoes, bacon, chicken broth, sugar and flour. Mix thoroughly and cook an additional 10 minutes. Meanwhile, cook rice.
4. Add chicken and cook 15-20 minutes or until chicken is thoroughly cooked.
5. Place cooked rice in the bottom of a casserole dish. Pour chicken mixture over rice and serve.
