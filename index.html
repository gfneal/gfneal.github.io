This site documents hand-crank methods for determining safe evacuation distances for ground testing of propulsion systems and sub-systems.

The document, “Q-D/Fragment Analysis Quicksheets,” summarizes the equations and tables included here and is available for quick reference after browsing this site.  A training package is also available.

NOTE: Excerpts from NASA-STD-8719.12 with Change 2, need to be updated to the 8719.12 Rev. A version.
